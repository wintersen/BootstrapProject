How does it work?

It's popular common knowledge that color and human mood have a scientific connection — calming blue, happy green, and aggressive red. Pink (specifically a rosy shade) when applied to test photos has a unique affect on people's perception of that image, turning normally sad photos into joyful ones and bland scenery exciting. Using this knowledge of the color spectrum and human psychology, we engineered a lens capable of filtering out all other colored light from the spectrum except for the most uplifting shades of pink. When seen through our patented lenses, your eye only sees a rose-tinged world, scientifically proven to stimulate production of endorphins related to rises in cheerful mood.   Learn more about our manufacturing process
